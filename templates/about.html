I'm Anthony Roy, a journeyman programmer with decades of problem-solving experience. The inspiration of MyAlmostMommy.com came from the confluence of two aspects of 2000's pop culture; ,The Wu-Tang Name Generator that gave Donald Glover the name Childish Gambino, and some particularly cringy dialogue from Gilmore Girls where Lorelai Gilmore refers to her father’s college sweetheart as “my almost Mommy.”

I created this page to practice full-stack programming with a real world website. You can find the code for this Python-based project on Github. Making this site was a ton of fun for me, and I hope you have fun playing around and discovering your almost-mommy!
