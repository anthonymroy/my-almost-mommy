The inspiration of MyAlmostMommy.com came from the confluence of two aspects of 2000's pop culture; ,The Wu-Tang Name Generator that gave Donald Glover the name Childish Gambino, and some particularly cringy dialogue from Gilmore Girls where Lorelai Gilmore refers to her father’s college sweetheart as “my almost Mommy.”

The reason for MyAlmostMommy.com was a need to practice full-stack programming wth a real world website. You can find most of the source code for this Python-based project on my Github

Making this site was a ton of fun for me, and I hope you have fun playing around and discovering your almost-mommies!
